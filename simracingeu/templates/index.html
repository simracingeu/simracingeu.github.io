Attention Simracers, Teams, and Communities Across Europe! Experience the thrill of international competition and camaraderie in the European SimRacing Collective Championship, an innovative, free-to-enter tournament designed to unite the continent's finest virtual racing talent and celebrate the rich diversity of our European simracing culture.

The SREU Advantage: A Pan-European SimRacing Movement

The SREU transcends traditional championships – it's a pan-European racing movement. Conceived to highlight the strength and potential of Europe's simracing scene, this platform brings together communities of all sizes under a shared, competitive structure — built on mutual respect, performance, and visibility.

High-Level Cross-Platform European Competition

Race on your preferred platforms with comprehensive support across the continent:

  • Assetto Corsa (AC)
  • Assetto Corsa Competizione (ACC)
  • Le Mans Ultimate (LMU)
  • Rennsport
  • EVO

No geographical barriers. No platform limitations. Pure European racing excellence.

Inclusive and Progressive Competition Structure

Embracing Europe's diverse talent pool, each participating country and community hosts three distinct competitive divisions:

  • ELITE Tier: Europe's top virtual drivers compete for continental supremacy.
  • CHALLENGER Tier: Talented competitors from across Europe develop their racing craft.
  • CLUB Tier: An inclusive European community where newcomers experience the thrill of international competition.

This structure ensures exciting rivalries and clear progression paths for drivers from every European nation and background.
